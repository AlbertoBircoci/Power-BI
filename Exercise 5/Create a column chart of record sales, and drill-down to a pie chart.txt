Exercise: Create a Clustered Column Chart to Compare Total Box Office by Certificate

Objective:
Visualize the total box office revenue for each certificate rating.
Use a clustered column chart to compare values side-by-side.

Steps Taken:
Used the Clustered Column Chart visual.
Dragged Certificate to the Axis field.
Added BoxOfficeDollars to Values and set the aggregation to Sum.
Enabled data labels to display exact revenue values above each column.
Applied formatting such as axis titles, label positioning, and color adjustments to improve clarity.
